# head_office_id

---

The unique identifier for the head office or legal entity.

In the context of AnaCredit, the **head office undertaking** is the legal entity whose legally dependent parts are the domestic part and any foreign branches.

See [Anacredit regulation:][anacredreg] for more details.

[anacredreg]: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/stats/ecb_statistics/anacredit/questions/html/ecb.anaq.180820.0047.en.html
