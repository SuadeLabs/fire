# orig_guarantee_amount

The **orig_guarantee_amount** is the original amount that was guaranteed at the origination of the facility

The original value is the monetary value of the protection item that was established at the date when the protection item was originally received as a credit protection. 

Reference: [AnaCredit Reporting Manual Part II , section 9.4.9](<https://www.ecb.europa.eu/pub/pdf/other/AnaCredit_Manual_Part_II_Datasets_and_data_attributes_201905~cc9f4ded23.en.pdf>)
