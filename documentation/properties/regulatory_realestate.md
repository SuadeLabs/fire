# regulatory_realestate

---

Parameter to determine whether the loan product meets all criteria to be classified as a regulatory real estate exposure in accordance with the applicable regulatory framework.

In the UK, the loan must meet all criteria specified in Appendix 2 of the PRA Rulebook, Article 124A “Regulatory Real Estate Exposures.”
