# ultimate_parent_id

---

The unique identifier for the ultimate parent of the person or legal entity.

The **ultimate parent undertaking** of a counterparty (if any) is the undertaking which effectively exercises, directly or indirectly, the ultimate dominant influence over the counterparty 

See [Anacredit regulation:][anacredreg] for more details.

[anacredreg]: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/stats/ecb_statistics/anacredit/questions/html/ecb.anaq.180820.0047.en.html
