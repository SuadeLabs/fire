# fiduciary_status

---

Identification of instruments in which the observed agent acts in its own name but on behalf of and with the risk borne by a third party

### fiduciary
Instrument in which the observed agent acts in its own name but on behalf of and with the risk borne by a third party

### non_fiduciary
Instrument where the observed agent does not act on behalf of a third party, and the risk and economic consequences remain with the agent itself.
