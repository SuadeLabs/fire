# rating

---

The risk rating assigned to a borrower based on the Internal Risk Rating framework.

The rating serves as a qualitative and quantitative measure of a borrower's creditworthiness. It is typically represented as a categorical value (e.g., "Low," "Medium," "High") and is utilized in the assessment of loan terms, pricing, and overall risk management strategies.
