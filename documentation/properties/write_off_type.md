# write_off_type

---

Classification of write-off events distinguishing between formally approved debt forgiveness and internal write-offs or write-downs where the legal claim on the borrower remains.


See (https://www.centralbank.ie/docs/default-source/statistics/statistical-reporting-requirements/credit-institutions/reporting-requirements-%28june-2010---november-2014%29/notes-on-compilation-%28rs2-rv2-rc2%29.pdf?sfvrsn=20&utm_source=chatgpt.com)



### formal
Formal debt forgiveness where the borrower legal obligation is reduced/removed

### internal
Internal accounting write-off or provisioning decision without extinguishing legal repayment obligation.
