# qualifying_property_amt

---

Total number of mortgaged residential properties held by the obligor across all lenders, excluding the primary residence.

To support the requirements under CRR Article 124E - determining whether a real estate exposure is materially dependent on the cash-flows generated by the property

### For a Natural Person
A qualifying property is a property that is residential real estate, is not the primary residence of the natural person and that is either: 
(a) security for a residential real estate exposure to the natural person, regardless of which lender has the residential real estate exposure; or 
(b) security for a residential real estate exposure to an entity which is created specifically to finance and/or operate immovable property, where the natural person acts as a guarantor to the exposure and receives the economic benefit from the residential real estate, regardless of which lender has the residential real estate exposure. 

### For an Entity
A qualifying property is a property that is residential real estate, is not the primary residence of the guarantor, and is security for a residential real estate exposure to the entity, regardless of which lender has the real estate exposure.
